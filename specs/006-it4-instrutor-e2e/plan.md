## Cross-Session Test Pattern

```text
1. loginAs(page, 'INSTRUTOR')
2. [assign workout actions]
3. await logout(page)
4. loginAs(page, 'ALUNO')
5. goto /aluno/meus-treinos
6. assert "Do Personal" badge
```
